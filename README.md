# 🏠 Quick House Building Guide

### 1. Preparation & Legal
*   **Site & Soil:** Secure land and perform a soil test.
*   **Permits:** Get building approvals and utility connections (water/power).
*   **Design:** Finalize your blueprints and budget.

### 2. The Foundation
*   **Groundwork:** Clear the site and dig trenches.
*   **Pouring:** Install the footing and pour the concrete (slab, crawl space, or basement).

### 3. Construction, Systems & Finishing
*   **The Shell:** Frame the walls, install the roof, and fit windows/doors to seal the "dry-in" stage.
*   **Mechanicals:** Rough-in the plumbing, electrical wiring, and HVAC (heating/cooling) systems.
*   **Insulation & Walls:** Add insulation and hang drywall (plasterboard).
*   **Exterior:** Finish the siding, brickwork, or stucco.
*   **Interior Finishes:** Lay flooring, paint walls, install cabinets, and fit final fixtures (lights, taps, toilets).
*   **Landscaping:** Final grading and outdoor paths.
